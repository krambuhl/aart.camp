**Acceptance criteria**:

1. **Script: `.claude/scripts/griot/use.ts`.** ESM, stdlib-only. Single behavior, no subcommand. Invoked via `node use.ts` (no args). Reads `learnings/rollup.md` from `process.cwd()` and emits one of three outcomes on stdout:
   - **Loaded** (file exists AND contains at least one `^## L-\d+\b` heading): `griot-use: loaded N learnings from learnings/rollup.md`, then a blank line, then the verbatim contents of `rollup.md`, then the citation contract block (see AC #2).
   - **Empty** (file exists AND contains zero `^## L-\d+\b` headings): `griot-use: rollup empty — no validated learnings yet` then exit. No citation contract block.
   - **Missing** (file does not exist): `griot-use: no rollup yet — run \`/griot-compact\` once captures exist` then exit. No citation contract block.
   - Exit code: **always 0** in all three cases. The result is informational; missing or empty rollups are not errors.
   - **Errors** (stderr `griot-use-error: <reason>` and exit 1): `learnings/` directory unreadable (permissions); `rollup.md` exists but is unreadable. (Genuine I/O failures only — file-not-found is the documented "missing" outcome, not an error.)

2. **Citation contract block** (literal text printed only on the `loaded` outcome). Must include:
   - The contract framing: "If you apply any of the learnings from rollup.md to a response — whether avoiding a pattern it warns against, using a pattern it prefers, or structuring output the way an entry dictates — end that response with `Applied: L-NNN` (or comma-separated: `Applied: L-012, L-027`) on its own line."
   - The discipline reminder: "Only cite a learning when you actively used it. The Stop hook greps the transcript for `Applied: L-\d+` and updates citations.json accordingly — padded citations poison that signal."
   - The tier-separation rule: "Only `rollup.md` is loaded at session time. Do not read `learnings/session-notes/`, `learnings/nightly/`, or anything else under `learnings/` during a session — those layers are allowed to contradict the rollup and are only valid inputs to `/griot-compact`."
   - The text becomes the new source of truth for the contract (the deleted SKILL.md was the prior source; copy the wording forward verbatim where reasonable).
